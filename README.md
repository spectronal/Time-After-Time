# Time After Time (Under development)

> *Every second is yours. And every second slips away.*

## About

Time After Time is a simple incremental game, but with a different purpose: it wants you to reflect on what you’re “clicking” while time passes. Each click represents a moment that will never come back. You can invest those moments into upgrades — but in the end, time keeps flowing the same way.

At first, it’s just a button. Then you realize time starts moving on its own. And then you want more. And more. But the feeling never changes: *it’s always passing*.

## How to Play

1. Click **"One more"** to gain "seconds"
2. On the right panel, trade your seconds for upgrades:
   - **Momentum** — time passes on its own, no clicking needed
   - **Déjà vu** — each click is worth double
   - **Sprint** — press the accelerator, +5 per click
   - **Fleeting** — +5 automatic seconds per tick
3. Use the **tabs** to filter between automatic and click-based upgrades
4. Click **Save** to store your progress (or wait for the auto-save every 30s)

## Technologies

- **HTML5** — structure
- **CSS3** — visuals (All the CSS for this game was and will be generated by AI **Claude**) 
- **JavaScript (vanilla)** — all game logic, no external libraries
- **localStorage** — local browser saving
